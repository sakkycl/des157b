Project Research: Comparative Analysis

The search for my kimchi - The Pudding

The search for my kimchi is a website created by Alvin Chang that opens a discussion about the Korean American experience with cultural foods like kimchi. The website utilizes on click, tap/press down interactions to take users on a journey of a Korean American trying to recreate his grandma's kimchi recipe. The website also incoporates data about food supply, Korean immigration, Korean restaurantes, and kimchi recipes.

Baking the Most Average Chocolate Chip Cookie - The Pudding

Baking the Most Average Chocolate Chip Cookie is a website created by Brien and Thomas to reveal their findings when computer generating chocolate chip cookie recipes. The website utilizes scroll and on-click interactions to walk the user through their three approaches: a mathematical average, predictive text algorithms, and neural networks. Their methods utilized information from recipe databases to generate each recipe.
