Capstone Project Topic Proposal

The topic I choose is climate change and my target audience are people of all ages, race, and genders because the climate crisis is something that affects all of us. I do not plan to work with a partner. I think a challenge for this topic would be creating a message that would be impactful for a large audience.

One mental mode I focussed on was emphasizing the consequences on climate change on our daily lives such as endangered foods. I thought of incorporate a utopian/dystopian narrative in which certain foods affected by climate change are gone and alternative foods become more common. I could visualize data about people's favorite foods or important foods in different cultures. I also thought of incorporating on-click interactions and overlays to display the data so the user can explore and engage with the site freely.

Another mental mode I focussed on was normalizing the use of public transportation. I thought I can incorporate a utopian/dytopian narrative if public transportation was our only or main mode of transportation, emphasizing what countries would be fine and what countries would struggle. I could incorporate data about the use of public transport in different countries. I also thought of incorporating a scrolling interaction in the website to highlight the data and allow users to explore the data at their own pace.
